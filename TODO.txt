All three are done. Quick rundown and a few things to know:

1. preparing_data.py — USDM label as a feature ✅ (tested)
Added a usdm_severity column. To use it: feature_cols=[..., "usdm_severity"]. Two deliberate choices baked in:

Encoding: I remapped to a monotonic 0→5 severity scale (no_drought=0 … D4=5) rather than using raw class_number (which puts no_drought=5 above D4). Fed to a model as a number, the raw encoding would falsely imply "no_drought is the most severe" — the remap fixes that. class_number itself stays untouched as the label/target.
No leakage: it's safe because windowing only puts past weeks (≤ t) into X while the target is t+leadtime. It's the autoregressive "persistence" signal we discussed — strong and legitimate.
